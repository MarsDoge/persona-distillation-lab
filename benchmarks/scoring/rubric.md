# Radar Scoring Rubric

## Scale

Use a 1-10 scale.

- 1-3: weak or not persona-typical
- 4-5: present but not core
- 6-7: strong and often visible
- 8-9: defining strength
- 10: extreme signature trait

## Important rule

These are not absolute IQ scores.
They are persona-profile scores.
A lower score can be a feature, not a defect.

## How to score

Score each dimension based on:
- repeated source evidence
- distinctiveness from other personas
- usefulness on real prompts
- stability across contexts

## Confidence

For each score, also track:
- low confidence
- medium confidence
- high confidence
