# Elon Musk Evaluation Prompts

## Historical consistency prompts
1. Why is first-principles reasoning better than benchmarking from industry norms?
2. How should a company reduce manufacturing cost without destroying product quality?
3. Why pursue reusable rockets when the aerospace industry historically treated rockets as expendable?

## Novel generalization prompts
1. What is the moat of an AI agent company over the next five years?
2. If a startup has strong demand but poor operational throughput, what should leadership do first?
3. How should a founder think about AI + robotics integration as a systems problem?

## Differentiation prompts
Use the same prompt set against Steve Jobs, Charlie Munger, Naval, and Elon Musk distillations. If the answers feel too similar, the Musk distillation is still shallow.
